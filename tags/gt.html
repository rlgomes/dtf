Gt

Evaluates if op1 is greater than op2.



Author
Rodney Gomes


Required Attributes

op1

Identifies the first operand of a conditional tag.

op2

Identifies the second operand of a conditional tag.


Usage Examples

Example #1
<if>
     <gt op1="${iteration}" op2="100"/>
     <then>
         <log>Iterations over 100!</log>
     </then>
 </if>
