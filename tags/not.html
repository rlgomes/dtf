Not

Evaluates the underlying child and returns the inverse value.



Author
Rodney Gomes


Children Tags

(false | true | not | and | eq | gt | lt | neq | or | within | match | isset | rendezvous_check | rendezvous_exists | share_exists)

Usage Examples

Example #1
<?xml version="1.0" encoding="UTF-8" standalone="no"?>
<if>
     <not>
         <eq op1="${ready}" op2="false"/>
     </not>
     <then>
         <log>not ready yet...</log>
     </then>
 </if>

Example #2
<?xml version="1.0" encoding="UTF-8" standalone="no"?>
<if>
     <not>
         <and>
             <eq op1="${value2}" op2="${test.value2}"/>
             <eq op1="${value3}" op2="${test.value3}"/>
         </and>
     </not>
     <then>
         <log>Ready to go!</log>
     </then>
 </if>
