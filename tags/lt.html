Lt
Evaluates if op1 is less than op2.
Required Attributes
op1
Identifies the first operand of a conditional tag.
op2
Identifies the second operand of a conditional tag.
Usage Examples
Example #1
<if>
   <lt op1="${iteration}" op2="100" />
   <then>
      <log>less than 100</log>
   </then>
</if>
